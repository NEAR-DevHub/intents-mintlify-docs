---
title: 'Check Swap Status'
openapi: 'get /v0/status'
---

This endpoint retrieves the current status of a swap using the unique deposit address. Use it to track swap progress and inform users of transaction status.

## Response States

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="PENDING_DEPOSIT">
Awaiting the deposit to the deposit address. The swap hasn't started yet.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="PROCESSING">
Deposit detected and being processed by Market Makers. The swap is in progress.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="SUCCESS">
Swap completed successfully. Funds delivered to the destination address.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="INCOMPLETE_DEPOSIT">
Deposit received but below the required bridge or quoted amount. Check refund address for returned funds.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="REFUNDED">
Swap not completed. Funds automatically returned to the refund address.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="FAILED">
Swap failed due to an error. Check refund address for returned funds.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Check>
When the status shows `SUCCESS`, your tokens have been delivered to your destination address!
</Check>

<Warning>
If your swap status is `INCOMPLETE_DEPOSIT` or `REFUNDED`, check your refund address for returned funds.
</Warning>
